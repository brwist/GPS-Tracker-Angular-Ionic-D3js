How to Upload GPS Logs using Your Phone
  • 1. Enable Personal Hotspot on your Phone. Click Here to configure.
  • 2. Add new Home network Profile (using Hotspot SSID and Password) on Trakkit Setup page.
    see How to Put Trakkit in Setup Mode (above)
  • 3. Now you can Enable Personal Hotspot to upload GPS Logs.
    (Very little data is used, will not impact your data plan)
Turn on Your Wi-Fi Hotspot
